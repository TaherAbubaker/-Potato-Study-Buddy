**a pomodoro timer. but make it potato.**

your new study companion is a little potato guy who bounces when you work, goes sleepy on breaks, and judges you when you pause. he's doing his best. so should you.

## 🍅 how to use it

**option 1 — the lazy way (recommended)**
1. download `index.html`
2. open it in your browser
3. click start
4. study

**option 2 — the git way**


## ✨ what it does

- 🥔 **potato companion** — bounces while you work, sleeps on breaks, goes full surprised-face when time's up
- ⏱️ **3 timer modes** — 25 min focus / 5 min short break / 15 min long break
- 🌀 **circular progress ring** — drains as time passes so you feel the pressure (affectionately)
- ⚡ **last 10 seconds flash** — timer turns pink so you know it's about to get real
- 🟡 **session dots** — tracks completed focus sessions, fills up every 4
- 🔔 **audio chime** — plays a lil melody when done, no audio files needed (Web Audio API)
- 💬 **potato speeches** — he says different things depending on what you do. some are mean (lovingly)
- ✨ **sparkle explosion** — shoots out of him when you finish. he's proud of you
- ⌨️ **keyboard shortcuts** — `Space` to start/pause, `R` to reset

## 🥔 potato mood guide

| what you do | what he does |
|---|---|
